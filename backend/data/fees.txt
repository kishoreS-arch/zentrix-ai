The fee structure of Sudharsan Engineering College is designed to be affordable and varies based on the course and admission category.

For Undergraduate Engineering Courses:
The approximate annual tuition fee ranges between INR 50,000 to INR 90,000 depending on the department and quota.

For Postgraduate Courses:
MBA and M.E programs have a fee range of approximately INR 60,000 to INR 1,00,000 per year.

Hostel Fees:
Hostel fees vary based on room type and facilities, typically ranging from INR 40,000 to INR 70,000 per year including food.

Additional Fees:
Students may need to pay for exam fees, transport, and other academic-related services separately.

Scholarships:
Government scholarships and fee concessions are available for eligible students based on merit and category.

Note:
The fee structure may change each academic year. Students are advised to contact the college office for the latest details.
