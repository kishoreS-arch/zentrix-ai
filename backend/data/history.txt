Sudharsan Engineering College was established in the year 2000 with the aim of providing quality technical education to students in rural and urban areas.

The college is located in Sathiyamangalam, Pudukkottai district, Tamil Nadu, offering a peaceful and academic-friendly environment.

It is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Anna University, Chennai. The institution has grown steadily over the years by introducing modern courses and improving infrastructure.

The college focuses on academic excellence, innovation, and overall student development. It encourages students to participate in technical events, research activities, and extracurricular programs.

With a commitment to discipline and quality education, the college continues to produce skilled engineers and professionals contributing to society.
