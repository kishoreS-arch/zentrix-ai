Students are expected to maintain discipline and follow the code of conduct within the campus.

Attendance is mandatory, and students must meet the minimum attendance requirements set by the university.

Proper dress code should be followed as per college guidelines.

Use of mobile phones inside classrooms is restricted unless permitted for academic purposes.

Students must respect faculty members, staff, and fellow students.

Ragging is strictly prohibited, and strict action will be taken against violators.

Students should maintain cleanliness and protect college property.

Participation in academic and extracurricular activities is encouraged for overall development.
