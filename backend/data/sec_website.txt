=== ADMISSION ELIGIBILITY ===
For B.E./B.Tech (Lateral Entry excluded):
- Open Category (OC/BC/BCM): Minimum 45% in 10+2 Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM)
- SC/SCA/ST: Minimum 40% in PCM (relaxation as per Tamil Nadu government norms)
- Admission through TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions) counselling by Anna University

Lateral Entry (B.E. 2nd Year):
- Diploma holders from recognized polytechnics
- Minimum 45% aggregate in Diploma (40% for SC/ST)
