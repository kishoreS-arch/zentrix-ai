Sudharsan Engineering College offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs under different departments.

Computer Science and Engineering:
Focuses on programming, software development, artificial intelligence, and data science.

Information Technology:
Covers networking, databases, cybersecurity, and web technologies.

Artificial Intelligence and Data Science:
Specializes in machine learning, big data analytics, and AI-based solutions.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning:
Focused on intelligent systems, deep learning, and automation.

Electronics and Communication Engineering:
Deals with communication systems, embedded systems, and electronics.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering:
Covers power systems, electrical machines, and control systems.

Mechanical Engineering:
Focuses on manufacturing, design, thermal engineering, and mechanics.

Civil Engineering:
Deals with construction, structural design, and environmental engineering.

Postgraduate Programs:
Includes Master of Engineering (CSE) and Master of Business Administration (MBA).
