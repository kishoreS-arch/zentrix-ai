Sudharsan Engineering College actively conducts various technical, cultural, and extracurricular events throughout the year.

Technical Symposiums:
Each department organizes symposiums where students can participate in paper presentations, coding contests, project expos, and workshops.

Workshops and Seminars:
Regular workshops are conducted on emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, and IoT.

Cultural Events:
Annual cultural festivals include dance, music, drama, and talent shows that encourage student creativity.

Sports Events:
The college organizes inter-department and inter-college sports competitions to promote physical fitness and teamwork.

Clubs:
Various student clubs are available, including:
- Coding Club
- AI/Tech Club
- Cultural Club
- Sports Club
- Entrepreneurship Cell

Special Events:
Guest lectures, industrial visits, hackathons, and innovation challenges are conducted regularly to enhance practical knowledge.

These events help students develop technical skills, leadership qualities, and teamwork.
