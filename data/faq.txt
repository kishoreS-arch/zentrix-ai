Q: Where is Sudharsan Engineering College located?
A: The college is located in Sathiyamangalam, Pudukkottai district, Tamil Nadu.

Q: Which university is the college affiliated with?
A: The college is affiliated with Anna University, Chennai.

Q: Is the college approved by AICTE?
A: Yes, the college is approved by AICTE.

Q: What courses are offered in the college?
A: The college offers undergraduate courses like CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, AI & DS, and AI & ML, along with postgraduate courses like MBA and M.E.

Q: Does the college provide hostel facilities?
A: Yes, separate hostel facilities are available for boys and girls.

Q: How are the placements in the college?
A: The college has a dedicated placement cell that provides training, internships, and campus recruitment opportunities.

Q: Are internships provided for students?
A: Yes, the college supports internships through industry collaborations and training programs.

Q: Is transport facility available?
A: Yes, transport facilities are available for students from nearby areas.

Q: Does the college support extracurricular activities?
A: Yes, students are encouraged to participate in technical events, sports, and cultural activities.

Q: How can I apply for admission?
A: Students can apply through counseling or by contacting the college admission office.
