The central library at Sudharsan Engineering College is a hub of knowledge and resources.

Resources Available:
- Over 50,000 volumes of books covering all engineering disciplines, management, and basic sciences.
- Subscriptions to national and international journals (IEEE, Springer, Elsevier).
- Digital library section with access to e-books, e-journals, and NPTEL video lectures.
- A spacious reading hall and discussion rooms.

Opening Hours:
- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- During Exams: Extended hours up to 8:00 PM

Students can use their college ID to borrow books for a specified period.
