The college has a dedicated placement and training cell that works to improve students’ employability skills.

Training programs start from the first year, including aptitude training, communication skills, and technical development.

Students are given opportunities to attend mock interviews, group discussions, and resume-building sessions.

Various companies visit the campus for recruitment, offering roles in IT, core engineering, and management fields.

The placement cell also organizes internships, industrial visits, and skill development workshops to prepare students for real-world challenges.

Top-performing students secure placements in reputed companies, while others are guided for higher studies or entrepreneurship.
